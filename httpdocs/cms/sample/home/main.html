Thornwood Cabinetry is a small custom cabinet shop. We also do renovations and new construction with an emphasis on kitchens and baths. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality cabinetry, and woodwork needs.

From small custom built-ins or pieces of furniture, to large kitchens and full home projects, and from the simple to the ornate, we work with you from the design process, all the way through to completion.
