A Story for Your Reading Pleasure (or not)...

Let me tell you a story, shall I? Do you have some time? It may turn out to be a long story. But then again it may not. I'm afraid that you may be bored. But then again, you may not. Will you take the chance? Will I?

I might start by saying that "it happened a long time ago." It did. But then again, I'm not sure whether that's relevant, because it happens every day, and will continue to happen, I suppose, till the end of time. But then again, it may not.
